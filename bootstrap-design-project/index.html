Why Choose Grey Dog Home Inspections?

Choosing the right home inspector can seem overwhelming. With your investment on the line and so many inspectors to choose from, how can you be sure that Grey Dog Home Inspections is right for you?

State of the Art

From our reporting software to our inspection tools, we are equipped with the latest technology to help us find conditions which will affect your investment. We are certified in Infrared Thermography, a technology which allows us to find moisture intrusion and energy inefficiencies that cannot be seen. We also use a high technology drone for chimney and inaccessible roof inspection.

We Are Thorough

Some inspectors rush from one inspection to the next, often missing critical issues. We take the time to ensure you are fully aware of conditions that affect your investment. We’ll discuss these with you and if you wish, we’ll review the report in detail with you.

We Work for You

As your inspection company, we have no financial interest in the home sale other than the fee you pay us to conduct the inspection. Maintaining strong, professional relationships with realtors is important for our ongoing business, but at the end of the day we are loyal to you. You can be assured that we will provide honest information about the condition of the home.

We Are Local

When looking to buy a home, it is important to choose a local inspector. We understand our territory of Fredricksburg and Stafford, and their unique characteristics. We are familiar with building standards and common issues that homeowners should be aware of in each respective area.
